Designer Studio

To maintain our edge in the dynamic fashion landscape, we leverage the expertise of a
global network of 20 experienced designers spread across 7 countries.

Where Visionaries Design Tomorrow’s Trends.




At Erve India, our Designing Department is at the forefront of fashion innovation. Our dedicated in-house team of visionary designers collaborates closely with renowned licensors and brands to craft unique and trendsetting apparel. This synergy of creativity ensures that every piece reflects both our distinctive style and the latest global fashion trends.

To maintain our edge in the dynamic fashion landscape, we leverage the expertise of a global network of 20 experienced designers spread across 7 countries. This diverse pool of talent allows us to bring the latest international trends to India in real time, ensuring our designs are both cutting-edge and relevant. From conceptualization to final execution, our design process is driven by a commitment to excellence, quality, and originality.

Playful Styles, Endless Adventures.




At Erve India, our Kid Licensed Fashion collection merges playful creativity with vibrant design, ensuring that every piece is both stylish and practical. Our in-house design team, in collaboration with leading licensors, crafts collections that embody the latest global trends while catering to the unique preferences of the Indian market. We are dedicated to creating apparel that is as comfortable and durable as it is fashionable, recognizing that children’s fashion must meet high standards of both quality and wearability.

We leverage insights from our global network of designers to integrate contemporary styles into our collections, offering young wearers clothing that resonates with the latest trends. By balancing global fashion movements with local tastes, we ensure that our designs are both on-trend and tailored to the needs of our customers. Each piece is crafted from high-quality materials that provide the durability required for active play, all while maintaining a focus on comfort.

Our commitment to innovation extends beyond design to include sustainability and ethical practices. We carefully select fabrics and employ techniques that minimize environmental impact, ensuring that our fashion choices are responsible as well as stylish. At Erve India, we strive to set new standards in kids fashion, making every day a fashionable and comfortable adventure for young ones.

Sophistication Meets Global Trends.


Erve India's Adults Licensed Fashion collection seamlessly blends contemporary trends with timeless elegance. Our dedicated in-house design team works closely with prestigious licensors and renowned brands to create apparel that caters to both modern fashion sensibilities and classic tastes. Each piece is crafted with a keen eye for detail, ensuring that our offerings are both stylish and sophisticated.

Our global design network plays a vital role in this process, bringing in the latest international trends to keep our collections ahead of the curve. By integrating these trends, we ensure that our designs reflect current global styles while maintaining a unique and refined aesthetic. This approach allows us to offer a diverse range of options suitable for various occasions, from casual outings to formal events.

The meticulous curation of our designs ensures that every piece provides a polished and refined look. We focus on high-quality materials and precise craftsmanship, guaranteeing that our apparel not only meets but exceeds expectations in terms of both style and durability. This commitment to excellence is central to our brand’s reputation.

At Erve India, we are dedicated to helping our clients make a memorable impression. Our Adults Licensed Fashion collection is designed to offer a sophisticated edge, ensuring that each garment stands out in any setting and allows individuals to showcase their unique style with confidence.
