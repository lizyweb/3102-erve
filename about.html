Who We Are

Erve India is a distinguished leader in the fashion manufacturing industry, with a robust legacy spanning over 37 years. The company specializes in producing a wide range of clothing and accessories, catering to all age groups—from infants to adults.
With a firm commitment to quality, Erve India collaborates with top-tier manufacturers across India to provide products that meet international standards, ensuring that Indian retailers can offer competitive pricing without sacrificing quality or compliance.

Erve India is a leading manufacturer of clothing and accessories, catering to a wide range of categories from infants to adults. With 37 years of global experience, we leverage our extensive expertise and economies of scale to offer Indian retailers competitive pricing that maximizes profit margins while maintaining stringent quality and compliance standards. Our longstanding presence in the industry highlights our ability to deliver exceptional fashion products worldwide.

Our approach to securing competitive pricing is driven by strong relationships with top manufacturers, enabling us to offer high-quality products at optimal prices. This strategic advantage ensures that our clients benefit from enhanced market positioning without compromising on product excellence. We adhere to rigorous compliance standards to ensure every product meets the highest benchmarks of safety and performance.

At Erve India, we pride ourselves on a dedicated team that includes skilled designers, an efficient warehouse staff, and a proficient sales team. We also tap into a global network of 20 designers across seven countries, bringing the latest fashion trends and insights to our clients. This international collaboration enhances our design capabilities and keeps us at the forefront of global fashion.

Our sourcing strategy is supported by three owned offices in Asia and one in Africa, allowing us to source merchandise from top manufacturers in India and beyond. This comprehensive network ensures that we provide the best in fashion, price, and quality, meeting the diverse needs of our clients. Erve India Private Limited operates as a strategic joint venture between Erve Buying N.V. of Belgium and Erve Beconnected India Private Limited, combining global expertise with local insights for continued success in the fashion industry.

What We Do


At Erve India, we are committed to enhancing retailer value by maximizing margins and volumes through high-quality apparel sourced reliably and competitively priced. Our vision is to produce valuable products with competitive pricing while embracing sustainable practices. We focus on manufacturing global products tailored for Indian consumers, utilizing sustainable fabrics and production techniques. Our goal is to extend our reach across all demographics and geographical regions of India, delivering excellence and innovation in every product we offer.





Key Areas

Key Areas

Design and Innovation

All products are meticulously designed in-house, supported by a global network of 20 designers across seven countries. This allows Erve India to stay ahead of fashion trends and introduce them to the Indian market swiftly.

Sourcing and Manufacturing

Erve India works closely with top manufacturers in India, ensuring the highest levels of quality and compliance. Their strong relationships with these manufacturers allow them to offer competitive pricing while maintaining stringent quality standards.

Sustainability

Erve India is committed to producing fashion items using environmentally sustainable methods. This includes using eco-friendly fabrics and production techniques that minimize environmental impact. Their focus on sustainability is integral to their vision of delivering value without compromise.

Compliance and Quality

The company adheres to global environmental and social regulations, ensuring that all products are not only high-quality but also ethically produced. This commitment to compliance is a cornerstone of their operations.

Retailer Support

By leveraging their economies of scale and deep industry knowledge, Erve India helps Indian retailers enhance their margins while offering products that are competitive both in price and quality.
