Design

At Erve India, our design ethos is driven by innovation and global inspiration. Our in-house team of visionary designers collaborates seamlessly with licensors and brands to craft products that set new benchmarks in fashion. Additionally, we leverage the creative prowess of a global network of 20 designers across 7 countries, ensuring that the latest international trends are brought to India with unparalleled immediacy.

Merchandising

For over two decades, the Erve Group has been at the forefront of sourcing licenses and brands across India, establishing a robust and well-connected manufacturing network. Our seasoned sourcing team and extensive industry knowledge ensure that we deliver licensed merchandise that meets international standards of quality, product excellence, and design innovation. From spinning to garmenting, our vertically integrated factories are adept at transforming our vision into exceptional products.

See Erve's Mission

Quality & Compliance

At the heart of Erve India’s ethos is an unwavering commitment to quality and compliance. We are deeply rooted in adherence to global environmental and social regulations, ensuring that every product we create not only meets the highest standards but also champions sustainability. By integrating the latest technologies and sustainable practices, we deliver high-quality apparel that reflects our dedication to both innovation and environmental stewardship.
